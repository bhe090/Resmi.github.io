Pancakes

These pancakes are soft, fluffy, and perfect for breakfast with syrup and berries.

Ingredients: Flour, milk, eggs, sugar, butter, baking powder, salt, vanilla extract.

Steps:

  • In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  • In another bowl, beat eggs, milk, and vanilla.
  • Combine wet and dry ingredients. Mix gently.
  • Heat a non-stick pan and add some butter.
  • Pour batter and cook both sides until golden.
  • Serve with syrup, butter, or fruits.
