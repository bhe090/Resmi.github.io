Shahi Paneer

Shahi Paneer is a rich and creamy Indian curry made with paneer and a royal blend of spices.

Ingredients: Paneer cubes, onion, tomato, ginger, garlic, cashew nuts, cream, oil, butter, garam masala, salt, turmeric, red chili powder.

  • Steps:

  • Sauté onions, garlic, ginger, and tomatoes with cashews.
  • Blend into a smooth paste after cooling.
  • Heat butter and oil, add the paste and cook for 5 minutes.
  • Add spices and cook till oil separates.
  • Add cream and paneer cubes. Simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  • Garnish with coriander or cream and serve hot with naan or rice.
