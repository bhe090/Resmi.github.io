Mutton Biryani

This is a flavorful and spicy Mutton Biryani recipe made with marinated meat and basmati rice.

Ingredients: Mutton, Basmati Rice, Yogurt, Onion, Tomato, Ginger, Garlic, Oil, Salt, Turmeric, Biryani Masala, Coriander, Mint.

Steps:

  • Marinate mutton with yogurt, turmeric, salt, and biryani masala.
  • Cook onions and tomatoes until soft.
  • Add mutton and cook until tender.
  • Parboil rice separately.
  • Layer rice and mutton in a pot, add mint and coriander.
