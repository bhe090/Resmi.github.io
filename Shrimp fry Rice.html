Shrimp Fried Rice

This is a quick and tasty Shrimp Fried Rice recipe made with rice, shrimp, and vegetables.

Ingredients: Cooked rice, shrimp, garlic, soy sauce, eggs, onion, green peas, carrots, oil, salt, pepper.

Steps:

  • Heat oil and fry chopped garlic.
  • Add shrimp and cook for 3–4 minutes until pink.
  • Push shrimp to side, scramble eggs in the same pan.
  • Add vegetables and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  • Add cooked rice and mix everything well.
  • Pour soy sauce, season with salt and pepper, and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
