Chicken curry

This is a spicy and creamy chicken curry recipe using Indian spices.

  • Ingredients: Chicken, onion, garlic, tomato, Corinender, Salt, Turmeric, Oil, Spices
  • Fry onion
  • Add chicken
  • Stir Fry for 10 min
  • Add Spices and tomatoes
  • Add some water
  • Then garnish with corinenders
